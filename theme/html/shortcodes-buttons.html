Shortcode Buttons

Small Button Size

 Sample of a shortcode button   Sample of a shortcode button   Sample of a shortcode button   Sample of a shortcode button   Sample of a shortcode button   Sample of a shortcode button 

Add small sized button with following shortcode. If no size is defined, then default is small.

[button size="small"]...[/button]

You can choose between 6 colors - blue, orange, red, green, black and white. If no color is defined, then default is blue.

[button size="small" color="blue"]...[/button]

Also there is a possibility to align your button to left or right side.

[button size="small" color="blue" align="left"]...[/button]

You can also choose to wrap style for button - paragraph or text. With paragraph wrap style there is possibilty to center the button. The default is always text, if wrap style is not defined.

[button size="small" color="blue" align="center" wrap="paragraph"]
 ...
[/button]
 Sample of a shortcode button with direction 

And you can also add directional arrow to a button with direction left or right.

[button size="small" align="center" wrap="paragraph" direction="right"]
 ...
[/button]

Big Button Size

 Sample of a big button   Sample of a big button   Sample of a big button   Sample of a big button   Sample of a big button   Sample of a big button 

You can add big size buttons. All formatting including alignment, centering and paragraph wrapping mentioned abowe also works on big buttons.

[button size="big" color="orange" align="left"]...[/button]
 Big button with direction 

Directional arrows work with big buttons aswell.

[button size="big" align="center" wrap="paragraph" direction="right"]
 ...
[/button]
